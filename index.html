About The Photographer

Clearwater Fine Art Images is the vision of the creative mind of Carli Clearwater. Carli began taking pictures as a child and has been passionately in love with the artistic expression in photography ever since. She loves to shoot a wide variety of subjects and has a very distinct vision of art and beauty. She hopes you enjoy viewing her images as much as she has enjoyed creating them.


Clearwater's Mission & Vision

"Clearwater Fine Art Photography exists to capture beauty and truth through the medium of photography."

Mission Statement:

At Clearwater Fine Art Images, our mission is to capture and showcase the timeless beauty of the world through the lens of fine art photography. We are committed to creating evocative and captivating images that inspire wonder, provoke thought, and evoke emotion. With a focus on quality, creativity, and authenticity, we aim to preserve the essence of our subjects and share their stories with the world for all to see and appreciate.

Vision Statement:

Our vision at Clearwater Fine Art Images is to be recognized as a premier destination for exceptional fine art photography that celebrates the natural world and human experience. We aspire to elevate the art of photography by pushing boundaries, exploring new perspectives, and embracing innovation while honoring the traditions of the medium. Through our images, we seek to foster a deeper appreciation for the beauty and complexity of life.

Nature





Nature photography is the art of capturing images of landscapes, wildlife, plants, and natural scenes. It's a popular genre among photographers due to the immense beauty and diversity found in the natural world.

Nature photography is a rewarding pursuit that allows photographers to connect with the environment, showcase its beauty, and inspire others to protect and preserve it for future generations. Whether you're an amateur enthusiast or a seasoned professional, there's always something new to explore and discover in the world of nature photography.



People & Portraits





Portrait photography is the art of capturing the essence, personality, and emotions of individuals or groups through photography. It's a dynamic and widely practiced genre that encompasses a variety of styles and techniques.

Portrait photography offers endless opportunities for creativity, expression, and connection. Whether you're capturing formal portraits, candid moments, or conceptual art, the ability to evoke emotion and tell stories through photography is what makes portrait photography a captivating and rewarding art form.



Urban Life





Urban life photography, also known as street photography or urban photography, is a genre that focuses on capturing the essence of city life and the human experience within urban environments. It involves documenting the daily activities, interactions, and dynamics of people in urban settings, as well as the architecture, culture, and atmosphere of cities.

Urban life photography offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse and dynamic nature of city living. Whether you're documenting the hustle and bustle of urban streets, the architectural marvels of cityscapes, or the intimate moments of everyday life, the art of urban photography allows you to capture the essence of the urban experience and share it with the world.



Nudes





Nude photography is a genre of photography that focuses on capturing the human body, typically in a state of undress, with an emphasis on artistic expression, beauty, and form. It can encompass a wide range of styles, from classical nude portraiture to contemporary art photography.

Nude photography is a complex and nuanced genre that invites exploration, creativity, and introspection. Whether you're drawn to the classical beauty of the nude form or the contemporary avant-garde expressions of the human body, nude photography offers a rich and diverse artistic landscape to explore and interpret.
